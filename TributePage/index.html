Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The People's President & Missile Man of India

Here's a timeline of Dr. Kalam's life:

  • 1931 – Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • 1954 – Graduated in Physics from St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli.
  • 1960 – Joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
  • 1969 – Transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
  • 1980 – Played a key role in India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • 1998 – Led India’s nuclear tests at Pokhran-II.
  • 2002–2007 – Served as the 11th President of India.
  • 2015 – Passed away while delivering a lecture at IIM Shillong.
“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.” — Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
